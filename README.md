Source-Blendr is a wholesale sourcing and product management platform that helps businesses centralize products from multiple vendor portals into a single workspace.
Instead of manually browsing supplier websites, copying product information, and maintaining spreadsheets, users can connect their vendor accounts, import products, organize catalogs, apply custom pricing, and build customer-ready price books from one platform.
Source-Blendr acts as the bridge between suppliers and resellers, allowing businesses to streamline sourcing, pricing, and product management workflows.
